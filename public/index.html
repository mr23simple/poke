Rarity Calculation

Rarity is calculated by multiplying the odds of a Pokémon's rarest traits.

  • Lucky Odds: 1 in 20 (from trades).
  • Perfect IV Odds: 1 in 64 (if Lucky) to 1 in 4,096 (if Wild).
  • Shiny Odds: 1 in 20 (Legendary) to 1 in 500 (Standard).

A higher score means the Pokémon is rarer.
